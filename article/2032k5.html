0. What is Tess?

Tess.IO is eBay‚Äôs cluster manager, with a vision of ensuring a world-class build, ship and run experience for eBay‚Äôs applications at scale, while ensuring high efficiency, security, and agility to the developers. Tess.IO leverages Kubernetes under its hood. Kubernetes (commonly referred to as ‚Äúk8s‚Äù) is an open source container cluster manager that aims to provide a ‚Äúplatform for automating deployment, scaling, and operations of application containers across clusters of hosts.‚Äù

Tess makes DevOps a breeze.

So, you don't have to setup k8s or docker on your server anymore.

Tess is not an new tech, it's a plus-version of k8s cli with eBay's own way.
